Welcome to
VIBGYOR

VIBGYOR Group of Schools is a renowned chain of K-12 schools with presence in Mumbai, Bengaluru, Mangaluru, Vadodara, Lucknow and Pune (Balewadi, Magarpatta and NIBM Road). In 2017, we added one more location in Pune i.e. VIBGYOR High - Yerwada at Commerzone near Pune airport. The proposed curriculum substantiates the promise of holistic education. The Sports and Performing Arts curriculum carries forward the assurance of overall development of the child. The sports activities include skating, cricket, fitness and games. Dance, speech and drama are some of the mediums through which the latent talent of the students is drawn out, encouraged and refined.
